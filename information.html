BALI

Temple

Uluwatu Temple: Cliffside temple with stunning sunsets and Kecak dance performances. Explore Ubud: Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest, Tegalalang Rice Terraces, and art markets. Beaches: Surf at Kuta, relax at Seminyak, enjoy seafood at Jimbaran, or visit Nusa Dua for tranquility. Mount Batur Sunrise Trek: Early morning hike for incredible sunrise views. Waterfalls: Visit Tegenungan, Sekumpul, and Gitgit waterfalls.

China

Li River Cruise

Great Wall of China: Walk along this iconic ancient structure, especially near Beijing. Forbidden City, Beijing: Explore the massive imperial palace and its rich history. Terracotta Army, Xi'an: See the thousands of life-sized warrior statues. Giant Panda Breeding Center, Chengdu: Visit and learn about panda conservation efforts. Li River Cruise, Guilin: Enjoy scenic views of karst mountains and rivers.

Paris

Eiffel Tower

Eiffel Tower: Climb or take the elevator to the top for stunning city views, especially at night when it's illuminated. Louvre Museum: Explore the world’s largest art museum and see masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo. Notre-Dame Cathedral: Visit the iconic Gothic cathedral (currently under restoration) and admire its architecture and stained glass windows. Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe: Stroll along this famous avenue and climb the Arc for panoramic views of Paris. Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur: Wander the artistic Montmartre district and visit the hilltop Basilica of the Sacred Heart for amazing city views.

Turkey

City

Hagia Sophia, Istanbul: Visit this iconic architectural marvel, once a church, then a mosque, and now a museum, showcasing stunning Byzantine mosaics. Cappadocia Hot Air Balloon Ride: Soar above the unique fairy-tale landscapes and rock formations at sunrise for breathtaking views. Pamukkale: Explore the surreal white travertine terraces and hot springs, and visit the ancient city of Hierapolis nearby. Ephesus: Walk through the well-preserved ruins of this ancient Roman city, home to the Library of Celsus and the Temple of Artemis. Blue Mosque, Istanbul: Admire the grandeur of this 17th-century mosque with its beautiful blue Iznik tiles and impressive domes.

Egypt

Nile River

Pyramids of Giza: Visit the world-famous pyramids, including the Great Pyramid and the Sphinx, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. Luxor's Karnak Temple & Valley of the Kings: Explore ancient temples and the royal tombs, including King Tutankhamun's tomb. Cruise the Nile River: Take a river cruise between Luxor and Aswan to see Egypt's beautiful landscapes and historical sites. Abu Simbel Temples: Marvel at these massive rock temples built by Ramses II, relocated to avoid flooding from the Aswan High Dam. Egyptian Museum, Cairo: Discover a vast collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts, including the treasures of King Tut.

Sri Lanka

Beach Area

Sigiriya Rock Fortress: Climb this ancient rock fortress for stunning views and explore its beautiful frescoes and gardens. Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, Kandy: Visit this important Buddhist temple that houses a relic of Buddha's tooth in the scenic city of Kandy. Yala National Park: Go on a safari to spot wildlife, including leopards, elephants, and diverse bird species. Galle Fort: Explore this UNESCO World Heritage site, a historic Dutch colonial fort with charming streets and ocean views. Ella: Hike to Little Adam’s Peak or visit the Nine Arches Bridge, and enjoy the lush tea plantations and stunning mountain views.

Venice

Main Area

Gondola Ride: Experience a classic gondola ride through the picturesque canals, allowing you to see the city from a unique perspective. Doge's Palace: Explore this historic palace, which was the residence of the Doge of Venice, featuring beautiful rooms and the famous Bridge of Sighs. Rialto Bridge: Walk across this iconic bridge, one of Venice's oldest and most famous landmarks, and enjoy the bustling market nearby. Murano and Burano Islands: Take a boat trip to these nearby islands known for their glass-making (Murano) and colorful houses (Burano).
